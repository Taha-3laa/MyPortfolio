Learnloop is a web-based project management system designed to streamline team collaboration, task tracking, and performance management. It provides a centralized platform for administrators to manage projects and users, while also empowering users to collaborate and track their assigned tasks.

  • Administrator can add, edit, and manage projects and users.
  • Administrators can create tasks, assign them to users, and track their status.
  • Comprehensive user profiles for both administrators and regular users.
  • Users can collaborate on tasks by adding comments and engaging in discussions.
  • File and image uploads for tasks and projects.
  • Admin can review completed tasks and provide ratings and comments on user work.
  • User performance reviews are displayed on their profile.
